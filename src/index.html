Why use ProtonVPN?

Security

Unlike other VPN services, ProtonVPN is designed with security as the main focus, drawing upon the lessons we have learned from working with journalists and activists in the field.

Privacy

With ProtonVPN your browsing history stays private. As a Swiss VPN provider, we do not log user activity or share data with third parties. Our anonymous VPN service enables Internet without surveillance.

Freedom

ProtonVPN was created to protect journalists and activists and break down the barriers of Internet censorship, allowing you to access any website or content.

Open source

ProtonVPN is open source. This means the source code is publicly available and anyone can verify its security. You can use our software with confidence.

Independently audited

As an extra layer of security and trust, all ProtonVPN apps undergo an independent security audit that inspects our software before it is released publicly.

No logs policy

ProtonVPN is a strict no-logs VPN service. We do not track or record your Internet activity, and therefore we are unable to disclose this information to third parties.

Access restricted content

ProtonVPN was created to protect journalists and activists and break down the barriers of Internet censorship, allowing you to access any website or content.

Share and download anything

Use our specially optimized servers for peer-to-peer (BitTorrent) to upload, download, and share files with high performance while maintaining your privacy.

Connect anywhere
